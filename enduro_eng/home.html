About us:

Profile:

We are Importers and Distributors of leading international brand motorcycle products in Southern Africa.

Our dealer network covers the entire South Africa, as well as neighbouring countries Namibia, Zimbabwe, Swaziland and Botswana.

Our suppliers are based in the USA, Italy, The Netherlands, Portugal, Poland and Japan.

With our roots in MX and Offroad, we are also growing in the Adventure Bike market.

History:

Symons Sport International got its name from the two Symons brothers,
Philip and Elmer Symons. The business was started by the two brothers in
2003. While Elmer was racing championships in the United States he
discovered good quality off road bike protection and got his brother Philip
involved in importing and distributing to all dealers in South Africa.

Since 2013 Hanco and Sonja van Rensburg are the owners and managers of SSI.
This family-based business in Cape Town, South Africa prides itself with
the high quality products and excellent service they provide to the Motorcycle Industry.
